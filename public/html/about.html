About the Owners

Rocky and Roxanne founded Get Straight Dog Catchers in 1972. Before starting the business, Rocky met Roxanne at the local saloon during an ultra-competitive dart throwing competition. Roxanne was impressed with Rocky's precision and they began brainstorming different uses for his accuracy - and that is how Get Straight Dog Catchers was established.


Our Services

We specialize in humane canine catching and relocation. We use the latest in dog net technologies including ultra-mesh diamond weave netting with fully telescoping carbon fiber poles.

We can catch any dog within 24-hours and we will relocate the dog so far away, we offer a 30-day guarantee that the canine won't find its way back to your property within thirty days. Satisfaction guaranteed.
